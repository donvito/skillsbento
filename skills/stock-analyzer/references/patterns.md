# Chart Patterns Reference

## Continuation Patterns (Trend likely to continue)

### Bull Flag
- **Setup**: Sharp rally → sideways/slight downward consolidation
- **Confirmation**: Break above flag upper trendline with volume
- **Target**: Height of flagpole added to breakout point
- **Signal**: Bullish (+2)

### Bear Flag
- **Setup**: Sharp decline → sideways/slight upward consolidation
- **Confirmation**: Break below flag lower trendline with volume
- **Target**: Height of flagpole subtracted from breakdown point
- **Signal**: Bearish (-2)

### Ascending Triangle
- **Setup**: Flat resistance + rising support (higher lows)
- **Confirmation**: Break above resistance with volume
- **Target**: Height of triangle added to breakout
- **Signal**: Bullish (+2)

### Descending Triangle
- **Setup**: Flat support + falling resistance (lower highs)
- **Confirmation**: Break below support with volume
- **Target**: Height of triangle subtracted from breakdown
- **Signal**: Bearish (-2)

### Consolidation/Range
- **Setup**: Price trading between defined support/resistance
- **Confirmation**: Break of either level with volume
- **Target**: Width of range added/subtracted from breakout
- **Signal**: Neutral until breakout (0)

## Reversal Patterns (Trend likely to change)

### Double Top
- **Setup**: Two peaks at similar price level
- **Confirmation**: Break below neckline (swing low between peaks)
- **Target**: Height from neckline to peaks, subtracted from neckline
- **Signal**: Bearish (-2)

### Double Bottom
- **Setup**: Two troughs at similar price level
- **Confirmation**: Break above neckline (swing high between troughs)
- **Target**: Height from neckline to troughs, added to neckline
- **Signal**: Bullish (+2)

### Head and Shoulders
- **Setup**: Left shoulder → higher head → right shoulder
- **Confirmation**: Break below neckline with volume
- **Target**: Height from head to neckline, subtracted from breakdown
- **Signal**: Bearish (-3)

### Inverse Head and Shoulders
- **Setup**: Left shoulder → lower head → right shoulder (inverted)
- **Confirmation**: Break above neckline with volume
- **Target**: Height from head to neckline, added to breakout
- **Signal**: Bullish (+3)

### Rounding Bottom (Cup)
- **Setup**: Gradual U-shaped decline and recovery
- **Confirmation**: Break above left side high
- **Target**: Depth of cup added to breakout
- **Signal**: Bullish (+2)

## Candlestick Patterns (Single/Multi-day)

### Bullish Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Signal |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Hammer | Small body, long lower wick at support | +1 |
| Bullish Engulfing | Green candle engulfs prior red candle | +2 |
| Morning Star | Red → small body/doji → green (3-day) | +2 |
| Piercing Line | Gap down, close above midpoint of prior red | +1 |

### Bearish Patterns
| Pattern | Description | Signal |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Shooting Star | Small body, long upper wick at resistance | -1 |
| Bearish Engulfing | Red candle engulfs prior green candle | -2 |
| Evening Star | Green → small body/doji → red (3-day) | -2 |
| Dark Cloud Cover | Gap up, close below midpoint of prior green | -1 |

### Neutral/Indecision
| Pattern | Description | Signal |
|---------|-------------|--------|
| Doji | Open ≈ close, shows indecision | 0 (watch next candle) |
| Spinning Top | Small body, wicks on both sides | 0 (consolidation) |

## Pattern Reliability

### High Reliability (wait for these)
- Head and Shoulders / Inverse H&S
- Bullish/Bearish Engulfing at key levels
- Triangle breakouts with volume

### Medium Reliability
- Double top/bottom
- Flags and pennants
- Morning/Evening star

### Lower Reliability (need confirmation)
- Single candlestick patterns
- Patterns without volume confirmation
- Patterns against the larger trend

## Pattern Checklist

Before acting on a pattern:
1. ✓ Is the pattern complete? (Don't anticipate)
2. ✓ Does volume confirm? (Breakout volume > average)
3. ✓ Is it at a key level? (Support/resistance)
4. ✓ Does the larger trend support it?
5. ✓ Is there a catalyst to drive the move?
